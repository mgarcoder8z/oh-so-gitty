What Are PCBs?

PCBs (polychlorinated biphenyls) are a Class I carcinogen used in school building materials between 1950 and 1979. They were banned by Congress in 1976 because of their substantial risk to human health. Since 2004, 180 Countries have ratified the Stockholm Convention to eliminate PCBs from use. Read More

warning

What Are the Risks?

PCBs have been associated with a long list of human ailments and are especially harmful to children, including cancer, thyroid dysfunction, diabetes, autism, lower IQ and disruption of the neurological, endocrine, reproductive and immune systems. Read More
