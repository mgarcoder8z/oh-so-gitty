How it Works

Choose a Design

Choose a charm design from our collection or use your photo or image and paste it into a shape. Write a message to personalize the charm.

Share or Send

Share your design or charm collection with others via a shared link or send someone a personalized charm.

Collect Them

There are two ways to display your favorite charms. Display them on a virtual bracelet or you can display your collection in a gallery.

Why Virtual Charms

Sometimes we want a keepsake to collect that doesn't clutter or collect dust, or wish to express thanks, good wishes or good luck to our friends and family near and far. What better way than a virtual charm that's easy to create and share online. Who would you like to send a charm to? The possibilities are endless...
