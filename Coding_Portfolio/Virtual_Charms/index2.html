Write Your Message

Simply write a brief text message and choose the way you want to reveal the hidden message (ie., fade-in, blinds, etc.). Both sender and recipient must have accounts with a private keycode.

Encrypt Messages

Encrypt the message and send. The recipient uses their unique keycode to unlock your message. No more prying eyes or shoulder-surfing while you are reading your messages!

Decode Messages

Recipients use their keycode to decipher the message. Once decrypted, messages can be hidden or destroyed after a set interval.
